📜 Server Rules

1.Be respectful

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, bullying, or discrimination will not be tolerated.

2.No cheating, scamming, or malicious behavior

Attempting to scam members, distribute malware, or exploit others is prohibited. Any suspicion of cheating reviewed by admins will result in immediate ban.

3.Keep conversations appropriate

No excessive profanity, sexually explicit content, or disturbing material.

4.No spam

no mic spamming of any kind

5.No self-promotion without permission

Do not advertise servers, streams, social media, or products unless approved by staff.

6.Respect privacy

Do not share personal information about yourself or others without consent.

7.Follow staff instructions

Moderators and admins are here to keep the community safe and enjoyable. Please follow their directions.

8.Use common sense

If something seems disruptive, harmful, or inappropriate, don't do it.

Have fun! Enjoy the community, make friends, and help create a welcoming environment for everyone. Failure to follow these rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans at the discretion of the moderation team.
